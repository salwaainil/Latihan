about them
aespa (에스파), stylized as æspa, is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment, consisting of Karina, Giselle, Winter, and NingNing. They made theeir debut on November 17, 2020, with digital single, "Black Mamba". The group is known for its unique concept that blends virtual and real-world elements, creating a multifaceted approach to K-pop. Aespa consists of four members: Karina, Winter, Giselle, and Ningning. Each member not only performs as a real-life artist but also has an accompanying virtual avatar, which interacts with their real selves and represents them in a digital universe. Aespa's concept revolves around the idea of "metaverse" and duality, with each member having a virtual counterpart known as "ae." These avatars are not just digital representations; they play an integral roles. In aespa’s narrative, Kwangya is an alternate dimension where the members’ avatars interact, embark on adventures, and face challenges posed by a mysterious antagonist known as "Black Mamba." The Black Mamba disrupts the connection between the real-life members and their avatars, creating a central conflict that is explored through their music videos and visual storytelling.


aespa members profile


Karina is a member of the K-pop group aespa, known for her stunning visuals, charismatic stage presence, and versatile talents in singing, dancing, and rapping. Before debuting with aespa, Karina trained under SM Entertainment and gained attention for her exceptional dance skills and striking appearance. She was also featured in a promotional video alongside EXO’s Kai, which garnered widespread attention and anticipation for her debut. Karina is known for her strong leadership and dedication to her group. She has an elegant and confident aura on stage, contrasted by her warm and playful personality off-stage. Fans admire her versatility in effortlessly transitioning between powerful rap, smooth vocals, and intricate dance routines.


Giselle has a mixed heritage, with her father being Japanese and her mother being Korean. She grew up in Tokyo, Japan, where she attended an international school and developed fluency in multiple languages. Training Period: Giselle had a relatively short training period compared to most K-pop idols, training for about 11 months before debuting with aespa. Hobbies and Interests: Singing, writing rap lyrics, playing the guitar, and listening to music, especially R&B and hip-hop. Debut: Giselle debuted as a member of aespa on November 17, 2020, under SM Entertainment, with the release of their single "Black Mamba." Distinct Traits: Known for her powerful rap skills and multilingual abilities, Giselle stands out as the only Japanese member in aespa. She also has a notable charisma on stage, balanced with a witty and friendly personality off-stage.


Winter trained under SM Entertainment for several years before debuting with aespa in November 2020. She gained widespread recognition for her clear and powerful vocals as well as her versatile dance skills. In the group's overarching storyline, Winter, like the other members, has a digital avatar named æ-Winter who plays a significant role in the narrative set in the virtual world KWANGYA. Her avatar adds a layer of uniqueness and complements her persona in the group’s futuristic and technology-driven theme. Winter's contributions to aespa, both in terms of vocal prowess and charismatic stage presence, have solidified her as an essential part of the group’s identity and success.


Ningning began showcasing her talent in singing and performing from a young age. Before joining SM Entertainment, she participated in various singing competitions and gained recognition for her powerful voice. Her passion for music led her to audition for SM Entertainment’s SM Rookies program, which she joined in 2016. Ningning debuted with aespa on November 17, 2020, with the group’s first single, "Black Mamba". Her vocal prowess quickly stood out, earning her praise from fans and music critics alike. Her ability to hit high notes and deliver emotive performances has made her a vital part of aespa’s sound. Ningning is known for her playful and cheerful personality. She has a strong connection with her fans and often expresses gratitude and love towards them. Despite her youthful appearance, her voice carries maturity and depth, contributing to aespa’s dynamic and powerful performances.
